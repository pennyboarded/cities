Las Vegas

Las Vegas, this city bills itself as The Entertainment Capital of the World, and is famous for its mega casino hotels and associated entertainment.The city is one of the top three leading destinations in the United States for conventions, business, and meetings.

Las Vegas is generally used to describe not just the city itself, but areas beyond the city limits, especially the resort areas on and near the Las Vegas Strip and the Las Vegas Valley. The city's tolerance for numerous forms of adult entertainment earned it the title of Sin City, and has made Las Vegas a popular setting for films, television programs, and music videos.
